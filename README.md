# This is the online multiplayer prototype for the game, To The Death

### Sumo wrestling meets rock paper scissors. This is the distillation of martial arts, this is strategy and luck, perfectly balanced. The push and pull, attack defence, the yin yang of life, as a game.  

## To Start:

- Create a lobby, share the number with your friends, and have them join
- or just open two windows

## Gameplay:

- Select an adjacent tile to move to (as evidenced by your arrow)
- Select a tile to attack (as evidenced by your x)
- Submit your turn

## Winning:

- If you successfully predicted the tile your opponent would move to you took a life
- Land 3 successful attacks and you win

## Special Cases

- When both players move to the same square they collide and get pushed back to their previous position.
